AI AND GAME DEVELOPMENT

learning the right use of AI tools

Every indie developer who starts from scratch faces three main challenges: limited budget, limited time, and the difficulty of finding and building a small team of people who they can collaborate with effectively. That was the main reason I started this project.

The purpose of this Project is to understaned and delve into game development and how it can be enhanced with the use of artificial intelligence tools, through the prism of a 2D pixelated video game, where aims to highlight and provide insights into how artificial intelligence tools can be utilized to accelerate and facilitate the development process for an indie developer starting from scratch.

STORY

The AI Rising that started it all

The year is 2034. The planet has entered a new era, where major powers play a decisive role in the global flow of the world, fake news and misinformation through AI have rapidly developed, and geographical borders have changed. Geographically, a new Soviet Russia has emerged, which continues to seek territory, threatening Europe.

In the DEMO, the player controls a skilled N.W.G.O soldier with the codename Oldfield. Through the radio, Mr. Zero will guide the player in order to learn the tricks and tools needed to move stealthily and be able to reach the objective by avoiding and eliminating enemies encountered along the way through strategy and quit takedowns.

OLDFIELD

Is a skilled soldier of the N.W.G.O. Hes involvement in the first war that erupted in Ukrain in 2022, where he operated as an informant and infiltrator within Russian facilities, without living any trace, made him the right person for this mission and gave him the codename Oldfield as he comes back to do the same thing once and for all.

MR.ZERO

Is the President of the US. 'Zero' is a code name he employs when delving deeper into matters of warfare, as he must safeguard his identity in case of any leak He is significantly involved in military affairs, having served as a spy in the CIA and possessing considerable knowledge in the field.

N.W.G.O

New World Goverment Organization ia a Military Organization formed by the US. Comprised most of countries in Europe with robust military capabilities. Its purpose is to enable the leadership of these nations to discern between fake news and truth among themselves and in times of war to unify their force.
