Top down CQB Shooter (Prototype)

This is a project that demonstrates an early build of a 2D top-down CQB shooter game. Its focus is on the playable character, from mechanics to modeling and animation. Below is a video demonstration and a breakdown of the steps involved in creating it (Modeling, Animation, Gameplay).

STAGE 1 (MODELING)

Creating Assets with (Aseprite)

I use Aseprite Aseprite for my pixelated project, because its a software made for this perpuse and it has a big gama of tools. I start by making the characters and in this case the body parts, because i'm gonna use a bone based animation.

STAGE 2 (ANIMATION)

Making Animation with (Synfig)

Because i want to give the characters a realistic human movement and hire frames, i choose to use Bone animation. For this reason i use Synfig a free tool for rigging and animate.

STAGE 3 (GAMEPLAY)

Giving life to the Game with (GameMaker)
