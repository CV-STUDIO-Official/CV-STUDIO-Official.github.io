ABOUT THE PROJECT

Custom clothing is a passionate project, where each design is crafted with care and genuine dedication. Every piece carries its own inspiration and story, from the creation of the graphic to the selection of the color palette, everything is meant to express something. Beyond creativity, it’s equally important for the artwork to be clean and high-quality, which I achieve by meticulously editing every detail in GIMP until it looks exactly the way I envision it.

THE FREE STATE
❮

THE IDEA

"With this T-shirt, I wanted to convey the philosophy of rural Texas (THE LONE STAR STATE). It represents the spirit of freedom, and how true freedom lies in one’s mindset rather than in a specific place or era. Anyone, regardless of where they live, can be a Cowboy, as long as they live freely and by their own rules."
